Our story, our passion

At Blat&Sucre we are more than a bakery and pastry shop: we are a story of tradition and dedication passed down from generation to generation. Since our beginnings, we have worked with the aim of bringing to every table products that are not only delicious, but also reflect the effort and love for craftsmanship.

Blat: bread like it used to be

Bread is our origin and our pride. Every loaf that comes out of our oven is a statement of intent: to recover the flavor and texture of traditional bread. We use selected flours, slow fermentation, and processes that respect tradition, offering a range that goes from white bread to more special varieties like spelt, rye, or multigrain.
