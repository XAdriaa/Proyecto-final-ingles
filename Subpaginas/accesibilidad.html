Accessibility

At RUDAVIDE XI SL, we are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all users, regardless of their abilities or limitations. Therefore, we have taken steps to comply with web accessibility regulations and have worked to offer an inclusive and equitable online experience.

We have designed our website with the following accessibility features:

  • Screen reader compatibility: Our website has been optimized to be compatible with screen readers, allowing users with visual impairments or blindness to navigate and access content without issues.
  • Keyboard accessibility: Users who cannot use a mouse can navigate our site using only the keyboard. We have designed the site so that keyboard shortcuts can be used to access different parts of the page.
  • Proper contrast: We have used high-visibility colors and adequate contrast between text and background to ensure that users with visual impairments can read the content without difficulty.
  • Adjustable font size: Users can increase or decrease the font size on our website to suit their reading preferences.
  • Alternatives for non-text content: We provide descriptions for images and videos, as well as transcripts for multimedia content, so that users with visual or hearing impairments can access all content on our website.
  • Clear structure: We have organized our website’s content in a clear and coherent manner, with descriptive headings and subheadings to facilitate navigation and understanding.
  • Assistive technology compatibility: We have tested our website to ensure compatibility with different assistive technologies such as screen readers and alternative input devices.

We are committed to continuously improving the accessibility of our website for all users. If you have any suggestions or experience any issues with the accessibility of our website, please do not hesitate to contact us.
